SEO is a powerful tool for getting your business noticed - but it also has the potential to be an expensive endeavor. Taking the time to research different firms and asking them the right questions can help you get the most out of your investment. Good luck!


What is SEO and why is it important?

In its simplest form, search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of making your website more visible in search engine results pages (SERPs). But why does that matter? Well, consider this:

The average person performs about 3-4 searches per day on a search engine like Google, and those searches lead to billions of dollars in online sales each year. So, if you want your business to be found online and generate leads (and sales!), you need to make sure your site appears as high up in the SERPs as possible. That's where SEO comes in.

An experienced and reputable SEO firm can help improve your site's ranking in the SERPs by optimizing your website's content, structure, and on-page elements like titles, metatags, and anchor text. In addition, they can also help with off-page SEO tactics like link building and social media promotion.
