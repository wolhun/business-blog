Factors to Consider When Choosing a Yoga Teacher Training

When it comes to choosing a yoga teacher training, there are many factors to consider. Here are some of the most essential:

1. The quality of the training - Make sure to do your research and read reviews before enrolling in any program. Find out what past students have had to say about the quality of the teaching, curriculum, facilities, etc.

2. The location - Ideally, you want to find a training that takes place in a beautiful and relaxing setting. Bali is the perfect place for this! Not only is it an incredibly popular destination for yogis, but it also offers stunning scenery and plenty of opportunity for personal growth and development.

3. The price - Yoga teacher trainings can be quite costly, so be sure to compare prices before making your final decision. There are often discounts available for early bird enrolments or group bookings, so be sure to ask about these when enquiring about programs.

4. The schedule - When selecting a yoga teacher training program, make sure to check the schedule in advance and make sure it fits with your own personal schedule and commitments. Some programs may offer part-time or online options which can be more flexible.

5. The teachers - One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a yoga teacher training is who will be leading the program. Make sure to read up on the instructors and get a feel for their teaching style before enrolling in any course.

The Application Process for a Yoga Teacher Training Program in Bali

There are many things to consider when selecting a yoga teacher training program, but the following list covers what we deem to be the essentials when making your decision. Location, style of yoga, schedule, teacher to student ratio, and cost are all important factors to take into account.

When considering location, many people dream of spending their days in Bali surrounded by beautiful beaches and tropical jungle. While this is certainly a draw for many people, it's important to make sure that the training program you select is located in an area that is safe and has good infrastructure. There should also be easy access to amenities like grocery stores and restaurants.

The style of yoga offered at the teacher training program is also an important consideration. There are many different styles of yoga, from vinyasa flow to yin or restorative yoga. Make sure you choose a program that offers the style of yoga that you are interested in learning more about.

Another important factor is the schedule of the program. Many teacher training programs runs for several weeks or even months, so it's important to make sure you can commit to the entire duration of the program. Some programs offer flexible scheduling options which may be a better fit for your needs.

The teacher to student ratio is also an important thing to consider when choosing a training program. A lower teacher to student ratio means that you will receive more individualized attention during your training.

Finally, you will need to take into account the cost of the program. Yoga teacher training programs in Bali can range from a few hundred dollars up to several thousands. Make sure you are clear on what is included in the cost before signing up for a program.
