Abercrombie Jewelry - Austin's Premier Destination for Estate Jewelry Transactions

At Abercrombie Jewelry, located in the heart of Austin, TX, we specialize in purchasing and selling exquisite estate jewelry, offering a unique service for those looking to buy or sell gold, jewelry, precious stones, and metals. Our expertise lies in providing fair and accurate appraisals, ensuring each transaction reflects the actual value of your treasured items.

We take pride in our extensive knowledge of various gems and precious metals. Our collection includes gold, silver, flatware, coins, diamonds, watches, precious gems like emeralds, rubies, sapphires, and semi-precious stones set in jewelry. We are particularly interested in high-quality pieces such as gold and silver bars, showcasing our versatility in dealing with diverse valuables​​.

Our expert appraisers are adept at examining diamonds and gemstones, focusing on size, color, cut, and clarity, and assessing for any imperfections. We are keen on diamonds set in engagement rings, wedding bands, antique diamonds, and diamond earrings or bracelets. In our jewelry assessment, we consider the type of precious metal and gemstones present and evaluate the resellability based on quality and condition. Furthermore, our evaluations of watches include brand recognition and condition, with a preference for renowned brands like Rolex, Patek Phillipe, and Cartier, especially those accompanied by their original box and papers​​.

We are passionate about buying gold, with a preference for 10, 14, 18, or 22 karats of pure gold. We also have a keen eye for antique silver flatware or jewelry, discerning whether items are plated or solid. Name-brand pieces naturally command higher prices in our assessments. Additionally, we are interested in acquiring platinum and palladium pieces​​.

At Abercrombie Jewelry, each appraisal is conducted meticulously to ensure fairness and accuracy. Our approach to each transaction reflects our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Whether you are looking to sell a cherished heirloom or purchase a unique piece of estate jewelry, we invite you to schedule an appointment with our professional appraisers for a rewarding and transparent experience​​.
