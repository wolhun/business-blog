Tips for Maintaining Your Garage Doors and Preventive Maintenance Tips

If you're like most homeowners, your garage door is one of the first things people see when they drive up to your house. That's why it's important to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your garage doors:

1. Regularly check the hardware: The hardware on your garage door, including the springs, tracks, and pulleys, should be checked regularly for wear and tear. If any of these parts are starting to show signs of wear, they should be replaced as soon as possible.

2. Keep it clean: In order to keep your garage door looking its best, it's important to keep it clean. You can do this by regularly wiping down the door with a damp cloth.

3. Inspect the weatherstripping: The weatherstripping around your garage door helps to seal out drafts and keep pests out. inspect it regularly for cracks or gaps that need to be filled in.

4. Lubricate the moving parts: The moving parts on your garage door, such as the hinges and rollers, should be lubricated regularly with a silicone-based lubricant. This will help them move smoothly and prevent them from rusting.

5. Check the balance: Once every month or so, you should check the balance of your garage door by releasing the emergency rope and then letting go of the door halfway up. If the door starts to fall, adjust the tension on the springs accordingly.
